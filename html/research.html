Design comparisons of EcoMart with other e-commerce websites.

Filter Bar Comparison

Website References

Amazon Filters | EcoMart Filters

Similarities

Feature Description
Search Filtering Both provide options to filter products by various criteria
Category Selection Organized filtering by product categories
Price Range Ability to set minimum and maximum price filters

Differences

Feature Amazon EcoMart
Filter Complexity Complex multi-level filters with many options Simplified eco-focused filters
Primary Focus General product categories Eco-friendly and sustainable criteria
Additional Filters Brand, rating, shipping options Material type, certification, environmental impact

What We Improved

  • Reduced cognitive load with fewer filter options
  • Eco-conscious filtering that promotes sustainable choices
  • Clear visual indicators for environmental criteria

Product Box Comparison

Website References

Etsy Product Card | EcoMart Product Card

Similarities

Feature Description
Product Image Main product photo displayed prominently
Price Display Clear pricing information shown
Title/Name Product name or title visible

Differences

Feature Etsy EcoMart
Seller Info Prominent seller profile and reviews Minimal seller info, focus on product
Product Focus Handmade and artisanal emphasis Eco-friendly and sustainable focus
Additional Badges Handmade, vintage tags Eco-certification, material badges

What We Improved

  • Clear eco-badges for quick sustainability recognition
  • Educational environmental impact information
  • Simplified design focusing on eco-credentials
